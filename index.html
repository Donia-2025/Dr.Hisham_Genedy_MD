Our Services & Specialities

Rhinoplasty

Comprehensive aesthetic and functional correction of the nose to improve cosmetic shape and nasal airflow.

Sleep Surgery & Snoring

Advanced surgical procedures targeting tissue blockage to treat obstructive sleep apnea and restore restful sleep.

Septoplasty

Correction of nasal septum deviation to resolve asymmetry, reduce congestion, and ease chronic breathing issues.

Ear Reconstruction

Microscopic middle ear operations, eardrum repair (tympanoplasty), and restoration of outer ear cosmetic shape.

Endoscopic Sinus Surgery

Minimally invasive endoscopic procedures (FESS) to treat chronic sinusitis, polyps, and congestion.

Tonsillectomy & Adenoids

Safe surgical removal of chronically infected tonsils and adenoids using modern, bloodless techniques.
